Privacy Policy — Little Brain Games

Effective date: 25 April 2026

KidLearn ("we", "our", "us") makes the Little Brain Games mobile app (the "App"). We respect your child's privacy and built the App to collect no personal information at all. This policy is short because there isn't much to say, and we commit to keeping it that way.

What we collect: nothing

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from any user — child or adult. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, phone number, location, IP address, device ID, or other identifier is sent anywhere.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or usage data is recorded.
  • No advertising of any kind. No advertising IDs are read or used.
  • No third-party software development kits (SDKs) are bundled.
  • No user accounts, no logins, no usernames.
  • No internet connection is required to play, and the App does not make network requests of its own.

What stays on the device

The App has a feature that lets a parent or child select a photo from the device's photo library to use as a custom jigsaw puzzle image. When this happens:

  • The image is read by the App from the device's local storage using Android's standard system photo picker.
  • The image stays entirely on the device. It is not uploaded, copied to a server, shared, or transmitted anywhere outside the App.
  • The image is not persisted between App launches. To use the same image again, you select it again.

Children's privacy

The App is designed for children ages 5 to 7. Because the App collects no information from any user, it complies with the United States Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws in other countries by default — there is no information to obtain parental consent for, because there is no information collected.

If you believe the App is collecting information about your child contrary to this policy, please contact us at the email below and we will investigate immediately.

Permissions the App requests

  • Photo / file access — only used when the user actively taps the "Upload picture" button in the puzzle game. The App uses Android's standard system photo picker, which lets the user pick one image at a time. The App does not browse, scan, index, or upload the photo library.

The App does not request internet access, location, microphone, camera, contacts, calendar, SMS, or any other permission.

Third-party services

The App uses none. It does not load code, content, or data from outside itself at runtime.

Sharing and disclosure

We share no personal information because we collect none. No data is sold, leased, transferred, or otherwise shared with third parties.

Data security

Because no information is collected or transmitted, there is no data on any server to secure. Files you select for the puzzle remain on your own device under Android's standard security model.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy in a way that affects what information the App handles (we do not plan to), we will update the effective date at the top of this page and post a notice in the App's release notes on the Google Play Store.
